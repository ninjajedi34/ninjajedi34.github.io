Our goal is to teach kids, as young as fourth grade, the fundamentals of basketball.

Who are we? We are the Sammamish High School Girls Basketball Coaches: Mike, Anna, and Becca. We want to start a program that allows young girls in the area to start exploring the sport of basketball. As well as get to know us coaches. We want to create a sense of community between the younger girls and the current high school players, who will be helping to lead a few clinics and camps during the year.

We will be offering a variety of different ways for young girls to come in and learn different skills:

  • Select Teams
  • Clinics with Current Totem Players
  • Three-Day Camps
  • One on One Skills Drills
